Biography

I’m Zhigang Fang (方志刚), a Ph.D. student at the Electronic Science and Technology Department of the National University of Defense Technology, advised by Prof. Renzhi Chen and Prof. Lei Wang.

My research interest is to solve critical problems in electronic design automation (EDA) with Large Language Model (LLM).
